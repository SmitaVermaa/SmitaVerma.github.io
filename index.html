I started as a Gold Medal engineer, went to IIM Indore, and spent 15 years inside some of India's most complex financial services organizations — insurance, wealth, bancassurance, pharma, medtech. I also write, coach, create content, and believe deeply that a multidimensional professional is a more effective one — not despite the range, but because of it.
